Programme

How the days flow

Tennis — every day. The heart of the retreat, not a line in the schedule.

01

The meeting

Transfer from Barcelona along the coast · welcome drink and a get-to-know lunch at the villa · first on-court session with the coach · sunset and dinner in the open air

02

The sea

Meditation at sunrise · breakfast · a day on a private yacht — hidden coves and swimming · afternoon court session · an unhurried dinner by the private chef

03

The game

Morning yoga · breakfast · tournament on the La Gavina courts · lunch in S’Agaró by the sea · recovery · mastermind with the business psychologist · secret dinner

04

The exhale

Breakfast · a hike along the Camí de Ronda by the sea · lunch at the villa · final session and stroke practice · transfer to Barcelona
